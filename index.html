Privacy Policy for Print My Recipe

This Privacy Policy explains how Print My Recipe (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and safeguards the information we may receive or process while providing our Chrome extension (“the Extension”). By installing or using the Extension, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Print My Recipe does not collect or store personally identifiable information. The Extension operates entirely on your local browser session to detect and format recipe-related content. We do not transmit any user data to remote servers or third parties.

How We Use the Information

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no usage or sharing of such information. Any content processed by the Extension (like recipe text) stays within your browser and is never stored on or sent to external servers.

Cookies and Tracking

Print My Recipe does not use cookies or tracking mechanisms. However, the sites you visit while using the Extension may implement their own cookies or other tracking technologies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites.

Third-Party Services

This Extension does not integrate with or rely on any external service that collects or processes user data. Any interaction with third-party websites or services is governed by the respective privacy policies of those third parties.

Data Security

Because the Extension does not collect or store personal information, we do not maintain any databases or cloud storage containing user data. Nevertheless, we encourage users to keep their browsers and operating systems updated to mitigate general security risks.

Children’s Privacy

Print My Recipe is not designed for or directed toward children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Any updates will be posted here with a new “Last updated” date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@printmyrecipe.com.
